what is is for?

Travis CI on your phone. Ever wondering if the tests are failing or passing while on the go? Want to check the logs? Save your favourite repos for even quicker access? It's fast, clean and easy to use!

thank you Travis!

This app wouldn't be installed on your phone if the Travis CI team didn't provide their awesome API. It's well documented, fast and pretty clear.
